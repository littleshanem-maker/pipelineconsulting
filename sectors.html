Gas Transmission Pipelines

High-Pressure Gas Infrastructure

Gas transmission pipelines form the backbone of Australia's energy network. We provide comprehensive project management services for the construction, modification, and integrity management of high-pressure gas pipeline systems, with deep expertise in AS 2885 compliance across all project phases.

From greenfield construction through to brownfield tie-ins on operational assets, our experience covers the full range of gas transmission works — including pipeline construction, valve station installations, metering facilities, and pigging infrastructure.

Pipeline construction management Greenfield
Hot tap and tie-in coordination Brownfield
Valve station construction Facilities
Pipeline integrity management Operations
Hydrostatic testing oversight Commissioning

Live Facility Operations

Brownfield & Operational Assets

Working on live, operational pipeline assets demands a different level of planning and safety management. We specialise in managing construction and modification works on operational facilities where production must continue — requiring meticulous risk assessment, permit-to-work systems, and coordination with operations teams.

Our experience managing works in live environments means we understand the practical challenges of hot work adjacent to hydrocarbon-containing equipment, isolation management, and maintaining safe separation distances while delivering construction activities efficiently.

Government & Public Infrastructure

Where pipelines meet public works

Australia's expanding infrastructure programs — from rail upgrades to freeway extensions — increasingly intersect with existing pipeline corridors. These interfaces require specialist project management that understands both the construction demands of public infrastructure and the safety obligations of pipeline protection under AS 2885.

Pipeline Consulting provides the bridge between government project teams, Tier 1 contractors, and pipeline operators — ensuring that pipeline assets are protected and that infrastructure programs are not delayed by pipeline interface issues.

Rail Level Crossing Removals

Pipeline protection and relocation works during grade separation construction, including lowering, encasement, and real-time monitoring of high-pressure pipeline assets beneath new rail infrastructure.

Major Road Upgrades

Managing the pipeline interface on arterial road duplications, freeway extensions, and interchange construction — coordinating third-party protection measures with road construction scheduling.

Urban Development Corridors

Overseeing pipeline compliance requirements as greenfield development encroaches on established pipeline easements, ensuring AS 2885 measurement length obligations are maintained.

Utility Relocations

Project management of pipeline relocations and protection works triggered by government utility corridor upgrades, water infrastructure projects, and drainage works.
