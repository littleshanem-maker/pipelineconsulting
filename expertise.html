AS 2885 Pipeline Systems

AS 2885.1 / .2 / .3 / .5

Comprehensive working knowledge of the AS 2885 suite covering design, welding, operation, and field pressure testing of pipeline systems. We apply these standards practically — understanding both the letter and the intent — to ensure your pipeline assets meet regulatory requirements throughout their lifecycle. Our experience includes safety management studies, threat analysis, and compliance assessments for both new construction and in-service pipeline assets.

Construction & Welding Standards

AS 4645 / AS 1554 / API 1104

Deep familiarity with the construction and welding standards that underpin pipeline and facility works across Australia. From gas distribution networks under AS 4645 to structural welding requirements and API standards for pipeline girth welds, we ensure construction activities are performed and documented to the correct specifications. This includes review of welding procedures, welder qualifications, and NDT requirements.

Safety & Environmental

Practical experience implementing safety management systems and environmental management plans within the pipeline construction environment. We understand the interface between general construction WHS requirements and the specific safety obligations that apply to works on and around high-pressure pipeline assets, including pipeline protection requirements during third-party construction activities.

Government & Public Infrastructure

Proven experience on
major public works programs

Pipeline Consulting has delivered project management services on some of Australia's most significant government infrastructure programs. These projects involve the critical interface between new public infrastructure and existing high-pressure pipeline assets — requiring meticulous planning, deep standards knowledge, and the ability to coordinate across government agencies, Tier 1 contractors, and pipeline operators.

Our role on these programs has included pipeline protection design review, construction monitoring, safety management planning, and compliance oversight — ensuring that pipeline assets remain safe and operational throughout major construction works occurring in their vicinity.

Rail Level Crossing Removals

Management of pipeline interface works during grade separation programs, including pipeline lowering, protection slab installation, and monitoring requirements. Coordination with rail alliance partners, pipeline licensees, and regulatory authorities to ensure pipeline integrity is maintained throughout structure construction directly above and adjacent to high-pressure pipeline assets.

Major Road Projects

Project management of pipeline protection and relocation works associated with arterial road upgrades, freeway extensions, and intersection grade separations. This includes managing pipeline encasement works, designing third-party protection measures, and overseeing construction activities in live traffic environments where pipelines cross or run parallel to road corridors.

Utility Corridor & Infrastructure Works

Coordination of works where new utility corridors, drainage infrastructure, and public services intersect with existing pipeline easements. Our experience ensures that pipeline protection requirements are integrated into broader infrastructure designs from the earliest stages, reducing costly rework and construction delays.

Pipeline Protection & Compliance

Specialist oversight of AS 2885 compliance requirements for all third-party works within pipeline measurement lengths. Including safety management study facilitation, construction monitoring plans, and real-time compliance verification to protect the pipeline licensee's interests and public safety obligations on government-funded projects.
