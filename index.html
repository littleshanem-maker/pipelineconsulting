Government & Public Infrastructure

Trusted on Australia's
major infrastructure programs

Pipeline Consulting has extensive experience delivering project management services on government-funded infrastructure programs where live pipeline assets intersect with major civil works. These projects demand rigorous safety management, detailed stakeholder coordination, and deep knowledge of pipeline protection requirements under AS 2885.

From rail corridor upgrades to arterial road duplications, we've managed the critical interface between new construction and existing high-pressure pipeline assets — ensuring public safety, regulatory compliance, and zero disruption to energy supply.

State Government Rail Authorities Road Authorities Pipeline Operators Tier 1 Contractors

Rail Level Crossing Removals

Project management of pipeline protection and relocation works during level crossing removal programs, coordinating between rail alliances, pipeline licensees, and construction contractors to maintain asset integrity throughout grade separation works.

Major Road Project Works

Management of pipeline interface works on arterial road upgrades, freeway extensions, and intersection upgrades — including pipeline lowering, encasement, and third-party protection measures in live traffic and construction environments.

Live Pipeline Infrastructure

Specialist management of works in proximity to and directly on operational high-pressure gas and liquids pipelines, including hot tapping, pipeline modifications, cathodic protection upgrades, and integrity management programs on government-regulated assets.

Safety & Compliance Management

Development and implementation of pipeline safety management studies, construction safety management plans, and AS 2885.1 safety assessments for government infrastructure corridors where pipeline and public works intersect.
